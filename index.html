Explore the Village

The Badkala Village is situated near Quantum University , jaysingha , India. Since it is a small place and many people in the village are below poverty line so they can't afford various facilities and one of them is education for their children. So to help them with the Education a team of Pahal from Quantum University visit there every Sunday and teaches various children and also have fun with them.
