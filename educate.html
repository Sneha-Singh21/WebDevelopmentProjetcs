Education System in the Village

Education , the most precious and valueable thing in everyone's life . It is very important to get education nowadays so that people can spend their life peacefully and easily.


  • Similarly if we talk about the Badkala village then there is 1 government school upto class 8th . In terms of cleanliness , school is very well maintained and clean. Children of age group 12-13 years study in that government school.
  • Record of teachers in the school is also good. There are 5 teachers and 2 Shiksha mitra who teaches different subjects to the children. They are also well educated and come from nearby places to teach in village.
  • Over all condition of education system in Badkala is fine . Some children goes to Roorkee ,Chhutmalpur for higher education and some children manages to get home tution.
  • In Badkala there was a private school but it was closed after the death of the Director of the school.

    • On the other hand , one team of students from Quantum University known as Pahal also visit their every Sunday to motivate and teach children as much as they can do , which is very good initiative by them.
