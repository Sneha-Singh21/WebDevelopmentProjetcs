INTRODUCTION

Badkala is a medium size village located in
Behat Tehsil of Saharanpur District,Uttar Pradesh
with total 110 families residing.

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Badkala Range village is 109207. Badkala Range village is located in Behat tehsil of Saharanpur district in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is situated 20km away from sub-district headquarter Behat (tehsildar office) and 46km away from district headquarter Saharanpur. As per 2009 stats, Habibpur Alis Tapoban is the gram panchayat of Badkala Range village. The total geographical area of village is 10868 hectares. Badkala Range has a total population of 911 peoples, out of which male population is 462 while female population is 449. There are about 110 houses in badkala range village.
